=== Oxy-Neon Blue Cursor Set ===

By: RIDDLER (http://www.rw-designer.com/user/70600)

Download: http://www.rw-designer.com/cursor-set/oxy-neon-blue

Author's description:

This is the series of the '''Oxy-Neon Blue''' cursors. Those cursors are designed with a blue color outline, keeping the interior of the cursors transparent. This cursor set was made by '''alexgal23'''. There are 16 cursor roles in this set. Two of them are animated, whilst the other cursors are static.
